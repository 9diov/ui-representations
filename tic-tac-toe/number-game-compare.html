Why are they the same game?
Arrange 1–9 in a magic square — every row, column and diagonal sums to 15. Each number occupies exactly one cell.

When a player picks three numbers that sum to 15, they've completed a line on this grid — which is exactly a win in Tic-Tac-Toe.
